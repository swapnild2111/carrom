{{ $data.year }} Total Slam Leaderboard

Each time a player scores a white slam or black slam, it is counted here. Slams are tracked separately for club matches and for state & YouTube matches. The player with the highest Total Slams is the overall winner.

How scoring works

Slam types

  • White slam Player starts the game and pockets all white coins, including the queen.
  • Black slam Opponent starts the game and loses their turn; then the player pockets all black coins, including the queen.

Match tiers

  • Club Slams in local club-level tournaments.
  • State / YouTube Slams in state championships and YouTube-streamed matches.

Finding the winner

  • Overall winner — highest Total Slams (white + black, all tiers).
  • White slam leader — highest Total White.
  • Black slam leader — highest Total Black.
