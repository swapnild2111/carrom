How scoring works

Slam types

  • White slam Player starts the game and pockets all white coins, including the queen.
  • Black slam Opponent starts the game and loses their turn; then the player pockets all black coins, including the queen.

Two record views

  • Total slams Club vs state/YouTube counts per player (Total slam tab).
  • State circuit Per-tournament matrix — which event each slam was at (White/Black slam tabs).

Finding the winner

  • Total slams tab — highest combined white + black across club & state tiers.
  • State circuit — highest total across named tournament columns.
